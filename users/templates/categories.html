SCIENCE CRIME BUSTERS

SCB is a forensic science event that transforms participants into crime scene investigators. Through a series of escalating rounds: including blood analysis, dental forensics, and case building. Teams work to unravel a carefully crafted mystery. With each clue, the investigation deepens, offering an immersive experience that blends scientific reasoning with deductive logic.

SIEGE

Siege is PsiFi’s ultimate engineering showdown, where participants design and build structures like bridges, gliders, and catapults using limited materials. Spanning three rounds, the event tests creativity, structural integrity, and precision under constraints. With each build, teams compete to combine practical engineering with innovative problem- solving in a high-stakes, hands-on environment.

RC PLANES

Participants will design, build, and fly their own remote- controlled aircraft, pushing the limits of aerodynamics and control. From lift-off to landing, it' s a test of skill, precision, and creativity. While our classic categories like Robowars continue to empower and inspire, the RC Plane competition opens a whole new runway for tech enthusiasts ready to soar.

SCIFINITY

Scifinity is a fandom-based science event that blends pop culture with debate, creativity, and problem-solving. From defending iconic characters to solving fandom-inspired mysteries, each round encourages humour, critical thinking, and multimedia expression, perfect for science lovers and pop culture fans alike.

DIAGNOSIS DILEMMA

This medical-themed event immerses participants in high- stakes scenarios, from emergency diagnoses to courtroom trials and bioinformatics crises. Teams take on the roles of doctors, legal experts, and public health professionals, interpreting medical data, building legal cases, and responding to life-threatening health emergencies. In this fast-paced, interdisciplinary challenge, participants combine problem-solving with real-world medical roleplay to navigate complex situations and save lives.

OPPENHEIMER’S ODYSSEY

Oppenheimer’s Odyssey is PsiFi’s flagship physics event, blending theoretical problem-solving, hands-on experiments, and debate. Participants tackle Olympiad-style questions, explore real-world applications, and defend scientific ideas, making for a rigorous yet engaging deep dive into the world of physics.

MATH GAUGE

Math Gauge is a fast-paced, interactive math event that blends logic, speed, and creativity. From mental math challenges and escape rooms to rounds like Math Bingo and Dart Formula, participants will solve puzzles, hit targets, and think on their feet. It’s a celebration of mathematics where numbers come alive through engaging, hands-on competition.

TECHWARS

Techwars reimagines computer science challenges through fast-paced debugging sprints, hardware-based scavenger hunts, and hands-on game development. Featuring real tools like the Godot engine and devices like Cardys, the event blends coding, creativity, and problem-solving in dynamic, tech-driven rounds designed to push participants beyond the screen.

ROBOWARS

Robowars is a robotics competition where engineering, strategy, and creativity collide. This year, participants will design line-following robots to navigate complex mazes, shifting focus from Arduino boats to autonomous navigation. From circuitry to code, Robowars challenges teams to bring ideas to life in a fast-paced, tech-driven arena.

THE LAZARUS MISSIONS

PsiFi’s astrophysics category that brings space science down to Earth. From observing celestial bodies to decoding the mechanics of the universe, this category is a journey through time, light, and gravity. Participants will explore astrophysical phenomena, engage in hands-on activities, and tackle challenges rooted in real astronomical observations and theory.
