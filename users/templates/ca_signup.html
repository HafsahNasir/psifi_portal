About LSS '25

The LUMS Science Symposium (LSS) 2025 is a four-day interdisciplinary research competition scheduled from August 7th-10th, designed to transform how young students in Pakistan engage with science. LSS 2025 is not just a competition; it's a launchpad for future research leaders that empowers high school students to become problem-solvers, designers, and innovators.
